Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

I take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use my Chrome extensions and services.

Information We Collect

Depending on which product you use, I may collect:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our extensions (e.g., saved tab groups, feature usage)
  • Account Information: Email address and subscription status for Pro features
  • Browser Data: URLs, titles, and organization of items you choose to save
  • Device Information: Browser type and version for compatibility

How We Use Your Information

I use the collected information to:

  • Provide core functionality of our extensions
  • Sync your data across devices (if enabled)
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Improve our products and develop new features
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important updates about our services

Data Storage and Security

I implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use Chrome's secure storage APIs
  • Cloud storage uses encryption and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Sharing and Third Parties

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. I only share data with:

  • Payment Processor: Lemon Squeezy handles all payment transactions securely
  • Cloud Storage: For cross-device sync functionality (encrypted)
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data to improve our products

All third-party services are carefully vetted and comply with applicable privacy regulations.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data at any time
  • Delete your data and account
  • Export your saved information
  • Opt out of optional data collection
  • Disable cross-device sync
  • Request information about your data

Children's Privacy

My services are not intended for children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify users of significant changes via email or through the extension.
