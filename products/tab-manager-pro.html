Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gives you 2 groups with 3 tabs each (6 tabs total) and 3 subgroups - perfect for trying the extension. Pro removes all limits and adds drag & drop, notes, and unlimited exports.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your tab groups are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to the cloud. Your data never leaves your computer.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Absolutely! Cancel anytime with one click in your Lemon Squeezy account. No questions asked, no commitments.

What are subgroups?

Subgroups let you organize tabs hierarchically. For example: Work ‚Üí Client A ‚Üí Design Files. Create unlimited nested organization with Pro.

Do I need an account?

No account needed for the extension! For Pro, you'll get a license key via email after purchase.

What if I have more than 3 tabs open?

The free tier will prompt you to upgrade to Pro, which allows unlimited tabs per group. Most users have 10-20+ tabs open, so Pro is designed for real-world use.
